2. R.C. Plane

The second tech event I participated in at my institute to develop my hobby was a RC Plane competition. In this competition, our team consisted of me (Ujjwal), Pavit, Hemang, and Vinay, the same team as XLR8. We adopted a unique design approach, creating a bat-shaped plane with a high wing span and area for enhanced flight stability and control. The uni-body design aimed at minimizing damage in case of crashes, and despite experiencing 3 to 4 crashes, the damage remained minimal. Our innovative design and successful execution showcase our team's creativity and problem-solving skills in the competition.

Our team won the Best Design Award in the event, and it was a lot of fun, except for a few times when I accidentally crashed the plane I was controlling. Because of this event, I learned a bunch about how planes are built and how they fly.
